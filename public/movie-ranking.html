The Best Movies According to Menwil

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Devil Wears Prada

I enjoy this movie because of Meryl Streep's witty comments.
The humour is very funny and the fashion is fabulous!

Chukkie

I enjoy horror movies and Chukkie is one of my favourites of all time.
Although the scenes are not realistic, I love sitting on the edge of my seat :)

Beethoven

I love dogs, so this movie is all about a dog takes over a house.
The dad was not happy with this dog at the beginning of the movie, but later on the dog (Beethoven) won his heart.
